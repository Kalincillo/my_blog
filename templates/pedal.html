Custom Guitar Overdrive

Objective

The main purpose with this project is to create a custom guitar pedal but not versatile, this is most focus in a warm sound

Red Llama

The famous guitar pedall red llama is used as a base and pre amp

Schematic

The schematic of the red llama is very simple just an inverter used as a pre amp that causes a natural overdrive that is responsive and sounds amazing with humbuckers or single coils

The guitar effect uses a 4049 inverter using two stages to correct the signal and provoke a higher gain but not too much

The circuit in the bottom is just to prevent unwanted noise and not drain the battery because they tend to drain when not used inverters are leave with no connection

SD-1 and TS9

THe other section of the pedal is based in the famous Super Overdrive (SD-1) from BOOS and the TubeScreamer from IBANEZ
